About Transport Sage

This transport system provides easier access to users taking public transport by allowing users to track
real time bus arrival timings so that they are able to plan ahead and make good use of their time and estimated
travelling time to each stop. Users are able to view the weather forecast and decide whether to bring along an umbrella
before heading out of their home. Every incident that happened would be updated to the app to notify users on the roads that might
have traffic jams due to the incident. Users are able to log into their accounts and top up their desired amount into their ezlink card.
This system aims to serve the users with easier access and convenience to public transportation.
